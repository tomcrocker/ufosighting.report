Investigate a sighting

Roughly 90% of UFO reports turn out to have conventional explanations. Before concluding anything exotic — or submitting a report — eliminate the common and mundane objects below. Adapted from ufos.wiki/investigate.

Check before you report

  • FlightRadar24 — live aircraft over your location at the time of the sighting
  • ADS-B Exchange — unfiltered aircraft tracking, including military traffic
  • Stellarium — which satellites, planets, and stars were in that part of the sky
  • Heavens-Above — satellite passes for your location, including Starlink trains and the ISS
