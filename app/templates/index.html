What this archive is

ufosighting.report keeps UFO sightings on the record. Every post flaired as a sighting on r/UFOs is archived here within minutes — including the original-quality photos and videos, which are preserved even after the Reddit post is deleted or removed. Roughly one in three sighting posts eventually disappears from Reddit; the record here survives.

Each sighting report is enriched for analysis. The report text is parsed for the time, place, object shape, movement, and duration of the sighting, and the location is placed on the interactive map. Camera metadata (device, capture time, GPS when the reporter consents) is extracted from the original files. And every geolocated sighting is cross-checked against the actual sky: satellite passes computed from historical orbital data, Starlink train detection, ISS visibility, and rocket launches whose twilight plumes are routinely reported as UFOs.

You can report a UFO sighting directly — the wizard walks through the questions investigators actually need answered, including the "five observables". Or start with the reporting guide and the guide to common misidentifications (Starlink, Venus, sky lanterns, drones) before deciding what you saw.
